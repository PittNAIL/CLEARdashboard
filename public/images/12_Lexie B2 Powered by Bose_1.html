Description

These award-winning, rechargeable, self-fitting OTC hearing aids are Bluetooth-enabled* and can be personalized to your needs. Lexie B2 hearing aids, Powered by Bose, pair with the Lexie app, the first self-tuning mobile app that’s clinically proven to provide audiologist-quality customization. With total end-to-end support, these FDA-cleared hearing aids come with a personalized Lexie app and on-demand access to our Lexie Experts®. And it streams phone calls for iPhone®. All for up to 80% less than prescription hearing aids.

Available in gray. For adults 18 or older with perceived mild-to-moderate hearing loss.

*Lexie B2 Hearing Aids do not stream music. Details in FAQs.

Streams calls for iPhone®

Enjoy the convenience of streaming your phone calls directly to your hearing aids with the Lexie B2s. They do not stream music or media. Streaming is not applicable for Android.

Fine-tune settings for any environment in real time

The Lexie B2s give you ultimate control of your audio settings. Fine tune treble, base and tone so voices and sounds are more natural. Save your preferred settings as environment settings that are quick and effortless to instantly adjust your hearing aids.

Clinical-quality hearing support in your pocket

Our knowledgeable Lexie Experts® are here for you. The easy-to-use Lexie app connects you with Lexie expert support via video, chat or phone, lets you adjust and personalize your settings, and gives you discounts and rewards for healthy hearing habits.

More control when you need it

The Lexie app allows you to adjust the direction your hearing aids will focus on sound. Select “Front” to focus in front of you, ideal for conversations, especially in noisy environments. Select “Everywhere” to listen all around – best for general listening, like walking outdoors.
